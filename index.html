White Noise

Space Engineering team from the National Technical University of Athens


About

White Noise is a space engineering team that was founded by NTUA students in 2017. Our main goal is to compete in engineering contests and share our passion about space.
During our first year, we designed and manufactured DrillSat that won the 1st prize in CanSat In Greece competition.
In our second year, we developed Projects Daedalus and Icarus, with our team becoming the first ever Greek team to reach the world finals of CanSat Competition. Daedalus ranked 4th out of 93 teams worldwide and Icarus won the 2nd prize in CanSat in Greece 2019.
This year, our team is conducting research on sounding rockets and will build Cronos, the first ever hybrid-fueled sounding rocked build in Greece. Cronos will compete at the Intercollegiate Rocket Engineering Competition 2021.
